There is a famous story you might have heard in which a meddling god meets his end at the hands of a forest dweller who mistakes his feet for a bird and shoots it, leading to the god bleeding out. This culminates in the end of not just the god, but his dynasty and the age of gods. Magic ends and society goes through several millennia before it reaches the highs of the past.

This is not that story. In this story the hunter missed and the Age of Gods didn’t end. The magic of the world never lost its potency. Gods ruled through their favoured.
